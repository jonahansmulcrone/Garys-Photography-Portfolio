About The Photographer
As a young boy I watched my Mother and learned from her as she tended her garden. My Father was an Army Aircorp Photographer and I watched and learned from him, and made prints in our darkroom.
I was trained as a newspaper photographer and worked for papers to record important events.
My Wife and I filled our yard up with beautiful flowers just like my Mom did. I taught elementary special education in the public schools for 30+ years. However, photography was my first love, and it has grown over the years.
Thank you Mom for the love of flowers, and thank you Dad for the love of photography. Without you, these beautiful photographs would not have been made.
